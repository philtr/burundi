Burundi, Africa

We'll be traveling to Burundi in Africa.

What We'll Be Doing

At this point our plan is to spend one night in the capital, Bujumbura, then travel 4 hours inland and stay 7 nights in Karusi where we will:

  • Train pastors
  • Teach women
  • Teach youth ages 12-30
  • Do Children's programs
  • Do relief work, evangelism, and love on people of the minority Batwa tribe
  • Have a possible soccer outreach.

The next Sunday we will return to Bujumbura for three days of local church meetings that will include programs for teaching women, youth, and children. We will depart for home on Wednesday, August 21st or Thursday, August 22.

An insteresting challenge in Burundi is that everything has to be done through a translator. Kirundi is their first language, with French and Swahili as their second and third.
